kYearLength

enum kYearLength {}

A type representing the length of a hebrew year.

There are 6 possible lengths (in days) that a Hebrew year can be. They are: 353, 354, 355, 383, 384, and 385 days. A year that has 353 days is short, a year with 354 is regular, 355 is long.

The 30 day discrepancy will occur in leap years because of the extra month. The length of the year is the same as if there was no extra month. (383 is the same as 353.)
